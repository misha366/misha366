## 💫 About me

I'm a passionate engineer who genuinely loves building software end-to-end - from the first product brainstorm to the moment a feature is live in production and users are actually clicking around inside it. Over the last 5+ years I've worked on everything from tiny internal admin panels to high-load enterprise B2B SaaS platforms and I still get the same little thrill when I see something I built start serving real traffic 🌍

What gets me excited:

- 🧠 Hard engineering problems that don't have a Stack Overflow answer
- 🏗️ Designing architecture for systems that need to live and grow for years
- 🤖 Bringing AI into real product flows (not as a buzzword - as a real feature)
- 🧰 Improving the developer experience for the whole team
- 👨‍🏫 Mentoring people and seeing them grow into ownership

I love clean, readable code, thoughtful architecture, well-named variables, beautiful APIs and the kind of UX that doesn't make users sigh. If a system needs to scale, ship faster, become more reliable, or just stop being "that scary legacy module nobody wants to touch" - I'm probably the kind of person you want on the team. 💪
